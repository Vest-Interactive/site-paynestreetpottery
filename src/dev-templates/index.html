Payne Street Pottery is a full service pottery studio and gallery located in the Clifton Heights neighborhood of Louisville, Kentucky. Get your hands dirty in one of our instructor-led classes, or rent studio space for unlimited access. If making pottery isn’t your thing but you want to purchase some original pieces for your home or as a gift, check out our gallery!
